# API Sync Troubleshooting

For Salesforce or CRM sync failures, support agents should confirm the affected integration, check the latest sync timestamp, verify authentication status, and collect recent error messages. Enterprise customers with repeated sync failures should be prioritized for technical support review.

# Renewal Risk Escalation

If a customer mentions renewal, cancellation, switching vendors, executive concern, or repeated unresolved issues, the ticket should be reviewed by Customer Success. High-risk accounts should receive priority attention from the assigned CSM.

# Billing Questions

For invoice, payment, charge, plan, or renewal billing questions, route the ticket to Billing. Do not promise refunds or credits without human approval and policy review.

# Onboarding Support

For setup, migration, implementation, or onboarding blockers, route the ticket to the Onboarding team. Include product area and customer tier when available.
